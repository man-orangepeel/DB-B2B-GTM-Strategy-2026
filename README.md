# DB-B2B-GTM-Strategy-2026

**B2B Go-to-Market Strategy for Découvre Bitcoin** — Plan B Academy · Bitcoin Business Track · April 2026

Market research report (15 pages, English) proposing a B2B entry strategy for [Découvre Bitcoin](https://decouvrebitcoin.fr), the French Bitcoin education company. Field-anchored on eight in-person interviews with French professionals (notaries, chartered accountants, CGPs, banks, PSANs) conducted 07/04–14/04/2026.

## Methodology in one paragraph

Mixed-method research combining desk research (regulatory framework — MiCA · DAC8 · ANC 2026-01 · continuing-education obligations · French training-financing system — and competitive landscape mapping) with eight field interviews of French practitioners conducted between 07/04 and 14/04/2026. The desk-level segment ranking was reshuffled by the field signal. Full methodology, criteria grid and confidence levels in [Appendix I](Appendix_1_Methodology.md).
